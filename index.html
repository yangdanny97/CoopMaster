Coop Master

About the Product

A smartphone app that allows backyard chicken owners to record and visualize data to be better aware of flock health.


Free to Download, coming soon to iOS and Android!


Often, problems in living conditions or nutrition for backyard poultry flocks will affect the behavior of the whole flock.


For example, stress might cause chickens to eat or drink less, while decreased egg production in hens might be a sign of calcium deficiency.


Our product allows small flock owners to create daily logs of this important data and view trends in flock behavior over time.


In the future, it will come equipped with even more tools to visualize and analyze the data, and external sensors for automatic data logging.

A screenshot from the first fully functional build of CoopMaster for Android

Implemented Features:

  • Record and save data on: temperature, humidity, egg production, food and water consumption
  • Visualize data to view trends over the last 30 days

Planned Features:

  • Hardware sensors for automatic monitoring
  • More ways to visualize data
  • Built-in statistical analysis tools
